About Our School

Our Story

Bright Star International School was founded in 2005 with a vision to provide quality education that prepares students for the challenges of the 21st century. What started as a small institution with just 50 students has grown into one of the leading secondary schools in the region.

Over the past two decades, we have maintained our commitment to academic excellence while fostering a nurturing environment where every child can discover and develop their unique talents.

Our alumni have gone on to excel in various fields including medicine, engineering, law, and the creative arts, making us proud in institutions across Nigeria and around the world.
